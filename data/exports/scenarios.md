## Detail

### Base — the grind — 55%
- **Thesis.** Autonomous substitution continues ~+2pp/yr; the ratio drifts into the mid-20s by end-2026. The US channel stays exhausted, allied displacement begins, and frontier chips keep lagging tools. Controls remain near-irrelevant to the aggregate number.
- **Confirming.** Ratio holds a 22-28% band; domestic semicap revenue grows YoY; chip share stays <16%.
- **Falsifying.** A >5pp single-quarter jump (discontinuity), or domestic revenue turning YoY-negative.
- **Exposure.** Cycle-neutral long domestic winners (Naura/AMEC/ACM) vs short foreign losers (AMAT/LRCX); the base case for the exposure ladder.

### Accelerate — the ceiling lifts — 25%
- **Thesis.** A credible domestic advanced-lithography milestone (SMEE / Huawei-linked SiCarrier) relieves the one un-respinnable chokepoint. Substitution accelerates, foreign vendors lose China faster than the cycle explains, and the ratio pushes >27% — the chip controls start to look moot.
- **Confirming.** Ratio >27%; domestic revenue YoY >20%; foreign-vendor China-% falling faster than total China WFE demand.
- **Falsifying.** Litho remains stuck at mature-node DUV; no domestic EUV/immersion progress.
- **Exposure.** Amplifies the base trade; adds litho-adjacent (Kingsemi de-bottlenecks) and pressures ASML China DUV harder.

### Stall — the chokepoint binds — 20%
- **Thesis.** The lithography chokepoint holds; a capex-cycle downturn hits capital-starved domestic makers harder than incumbents; allied controls tighten and force reliance shifts. The ratio stalls below ~22% and the substitution story pauses.
- **Confirming.** Ratio flat-to-down; domestic revenue YoY-negative; allied (NL/Japan) controls tighten materially.
- **Falsifying.** Domestic revenue keeps compounding through a cycle trough.
- **Exposure.** Unwinds the cycle-neutral trade; foreign incumbents (AMAT/LRCX/KLA) recover China share; the theme goes dormant.
